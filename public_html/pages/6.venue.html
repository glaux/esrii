Venue - Rigshospitalet

The 6th esrii conference will be held in Copenhagen at Rigshospitalet.
Rigshospitalet is the biggest hospital in Denmark, located between Nørrebro and Østerbro and right next to the beautiful Fælledparken.

Rigshospitalet
Blegdamsvej 9
2100 København

The presentations on Thursday the 5th will take place in Auditorium 1 and 2 (easiest to find through entrance 2 or 44).
The presentations on Friday the 6th will take place in Auditorium 1 and in Psychiatric Center Copenhagens Auditorium (Building 61).

How to get there

The easiest way to find your way around Copenhagen (and the rest of Denmark) is to use the excellent travel planner Rejseplanen either directly on the web or via the app from App Store or Google Play.

General directions

From the airport: take Metro line 2 (direction Vanløse) to Nørreport station. At Nørreport you change to one of the following busses: 42 (direction ballerup), 6A (direction Buddinge) or 150S (direction Gl. Holte Øverødvej).

From Copenhagen Central Station: take 6A from Hovedbanegården (Vesterbrogade) (direction Buddinge).

All busses stop at Rigshospitalet Syd (Tagensvej). From here you can walk to the venue using the map above. If you arrive between 8.40 and 9:40 on the 5th there will be volunteers to guide you to the auditoriums.
