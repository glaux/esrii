Stephanie won the superspot nomination and will be presenting friday morning:

Understanding and Improving Mental Health App Engagement: A Deep Dive into User Experience Case Studies

Digital interventions for psychosis are often described to be feasible, but why do people use them? This presentation will describe a small series of case studies embedded within a clinical trial that use both quantitative and qualitative data to theorise complexities that underpin intervention (dis)engagement.
